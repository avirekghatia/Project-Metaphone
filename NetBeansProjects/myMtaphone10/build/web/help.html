How To Use?




This website is the demonstration for the project Hindi Metaphone .

The website uses approximation to resolve the spelling ambiguities and searches the keywords within the entire documents.

Often one person is not able to recall the exact title of the song and can only remember some of the words of the lyric. The website helps in getting the title of the song on the basis of the in between lyrics.
